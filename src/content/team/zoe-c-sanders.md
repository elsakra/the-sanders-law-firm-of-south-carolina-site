Zoe Sanders is a criminal, civil and regulatory lawyer with broad and in depth experience helping individuals and entities through complex issues. She has represented such diverse individuals as the comedian Stephen Colbert, U.S. Attorney General John Ashcroft and a battered spouse charged with the murder of her husband. She represents individuals who have been denied disability benefits governed by an employee disability plan. She represents nationwide corporations in the areas of food and beverage and hospitality licensing. Zoe is a past president of the National Association of Women Lawyers. She was awarded The State Newspaper's "Top 20 under 40 Achievement Award". She is a former law clerk to U.S. Federal District Court judge Matthew J. Perry. She is a former state prosecutor. She is a member of the Fourth Circuit Judicial Conference and a board member of the South Carolina Administrative and Regulatory Law Association.
